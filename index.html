Advanced Donor Insights Tool

Upload a Zeffy payment export to generate campaign results, donor segments, monthly giving prospects, referral summaries, annual campaign-window analysis, and practical follow-up lists. Processing happens in this browser session; donor data is not sent to a server or saved by default.

Upload Zeffy export Local-only processing by default CSV and XLSX supported

1. Upload Zeffy export

Choose a Zeffy payment export in .xlsx or .csv format. The app will preview the columns and suggest field mappings before generating insights.

Drop your file here, or select it from your computer

Recommended fields include Payment Date, Total Amount, Payment Status, Campaign Title, First Name, Last Name, Email, Recurring Status, Refund Amount, Eligible Amount, Rate Title, Ticket #, Fund, and any referral or recognition fields.

2. Analysis controls

Adjust thresholds or inclusion rules before generating the dashboard. Defaults are set for practical nonprofit follow-up planning.

Free registrations are kept as engagement signals, but are not counted as donation revenue. Refunded or failed transactions are excluded from revenue by default.

3. Confirm field mapping

The tool has guessed which uploaded columns match the analysis fields. Review the required fields first, then adjust optional fields if needed.

Data quality notes

Use these notes to interpret the dashboard responsibly.

Other donation systems. Zeffy exports may not include donations received through CanadaHelps, Keela, mail, cheque, cash, securities, or other platforms.
Monthly giving may be understated. Recurring support outside Zeffy will not be detected in this file.
Missing emails limit follow-up. Records without usable email addresses can still show engagement, but are harder to act on.
Free registrations are engagement, not revenue. They are useful for relationship-building, but should not be interpreted as donation revenue.
Campaign categories are suggestions. Staff should review and override categories where form names are ambiguous.
Referral tracking depends on form design. It only works when Zeffy includes a referral question or equivalent custom field.
Planning tool, not accounting system. This dashboard does not replace formal accounting, reconciliation, tax receipting, or audit records.
Privacy default. Uploaded data is processed in the browser session and is not written to localStorage by this prototype.
